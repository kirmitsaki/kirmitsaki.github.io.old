Overview

The Project

We met with the founders of what was to become Aladdin as the seed of a powerful idea was being conceived whose ultimate aim was to prevent diabetes. Together, through workshops and ongoing collaboration, we have taken this early stage concept and evolved it to what it is today:

A digital healthcare ecosystem that will change the way society approaches healthcare, refocusing the industry toward preventative medicine, where trust and efficiency are at the forefront.

The Vision

“To improve global disease identification and well-being“

GOAL 1: PREVENTATIVE HEALTHCARE - Applying cutting edge big data and machine learning technology to mine the wealth of user data to create proprietary preventative care applications.

GOAL 2: HEALTHCARE ECOSYSTEM INFRASTRUCTURE - To build a blockchain based ecosystem to cut waste and bring transparency and trust to the healthcare sector globally – sharing and automating data and related information.

GOAL 3: HEALTHCARE APP AND MARKETPLACE - To enhance the interaction between healthcare providers and patients, as well as offer a convenient route to discounts through the marketplace.

Our role within Aladdin

Elemental Concept are working in partnership with Aladdin. Our technologists are using their expertise to:

  • Build the blockchain infrastructure that will enable the ecosystem
  • Design and build all platforms within the ecosystem
  • Create the world’s largest health data repository, which using AI and machine learning technology, will facilitate the development of preventative care

Product Discovery

The first step was a series of product discovery workshops, establishing the fundamental concepts of the business problem and scoping the solution.

These sessions produced a product story map, defining the key features and functionality of the solution, which was prioritized to deliver an MVP to market quickly and efficiently.

The workshop’s success moved us on to the next stage in the evolution of this solution – Business Consultancy.

Business Consultancy

To validate the agreed solution, in partnership with Aladdin, our consultants developed the business plan, crafting multiple slide decks to prepare for investor funding.

This covered industry, competitor and technology analysis to ensure we could deliver the right solution to address the problems identified facing the healthcare industry.

UX/UI Design

We are design led from Product Discovery and beyond. The UX team ensures the end-user shapes the design and ongoing development of the product.

Our team created prototypes and interface designs for all of the Aladdin platforms and applications.

Product Development

With a validated and funded solution, we started the build, adopting lean agile principles with a scrum team delivering to weekly sprints.

Brand Creation

The Aladdin Application

Enables patients and healthcare professionals to easily access, upload and analyse medical records, including images, x-rays, scans and voice data through an integrated web and mobile ecosystem.

Security with Blockchain

Aladdin uses blockchain technology to store the data and provide security to ensure its true independence, permanence, credibility and ultimate safety.

Technologies
