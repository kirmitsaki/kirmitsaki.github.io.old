story

Hi! I'm Rachel, a senior product designer based in London, UK with deep roots in the information technology and service industries. I have more than 8 years of experience helping multidisciplinary teams to define and implement projects, find efficiencies, identify risks and drive customer engagement for sustainable growth. My process aims in delivering clearly defined experiences to customers by combining strategy with execution in practical and easy-to-build solutions that balance aesthetics and functionality.

My bachelor studies were focused on Information and Communication Technologies while my postgraduate equipped me to handle the complex tasks and processes associated with interdisciplinary business subjects - such as ethical decision making, global business values, intercultural business practices, operational research, corporate communications and employee motivation.

We are the craftsmen that implement what users want and companies need. We are almost magicians operating secretly within this astounding digital landscape.
