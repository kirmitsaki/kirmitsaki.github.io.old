The Raven - Poem narrative site design

A design exercise focused on creating a site that “narrates” Edgar Alan Poe’s “The Raven”.

Details

I was experimenting with CSS effects to find a way of capturing the poem’s supernatural atmosphere in a website.

By using scrolling parallax background effects the background-image changes as Poe’s poem “The Raven” unfolds. Reaching the to bottom, the background-image fades into one that shows the raven fly in the sky instead of sitting on the fence.
